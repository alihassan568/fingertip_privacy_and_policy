# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 29, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how Fingertip (FIGTIP) ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect information to provide better services to all our users. The types of information we collect include:

### Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Information**: Your name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.
- **Profile Information**: Your profile picture, preferences, and other details you add to your user profile.
- **Booking & Marketplace Information**: Details about products you book, store interactions, and preferences within the App.
- **Communication Information**: Messages you send to stores or to our customer support.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Precise Location Data**: We collect your precise location information with your permission to show you nearby malls, stores, and relevant promotions. This information is collected while the App is in use.
- **Device Identifiers**: We collect unique device identifiers (such as Android ID or Advertising ID) to improve app performance and for security purposes.
- **Device Information**: Information about the device model, operating system version, and mobile network.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with our App, such as the stores you view and features you use.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our marketplace services.
- Process and manage your bookings and store interactions.
- Show you relevant malls, stores, and promotions based on your location.
- Communicate with you about your account, bookings, and promotional offers.
- Facilitate communication between you and store owners.
- Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.
- Analyze usage patterns to improve user experience.

## 3. Data Safety & Sharing
We are committed to protecting your data. We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information with:
- **Store Owners**: To facilitate bookings and transactions you initiate.
- **Service Providers**: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., cloud hosting, analytics).
- **Legal Compliance**: When required by law or to protect our rights.

## 4. Data Retention & Deletion
We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services.

### Account Deletion
You have the right to delete your account and associated data at any time. You can:
1. Navigate to **Settings > Account > Delete Account** within the App.
2. Contact our support team at **support@fingertip.com** to request data deletion.

Once requested, your account and all non-transactional personal data will be permanently deleted from our records within 30 days.

## 5. Your Choices and Rights

### Location Information
You can control the App's access to your location through your device's privacy settings. Note that disabling location will impact your ability to find nearby stores and malls.

### Permissions
The App may request permissions for:
- **Location**: To find nearby stores.
- **Camera/Photos**: To upload profile pictures or scan QR codes.
- **Storage**: To cache app data for better performance.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Security
We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, disclosure, or destruction.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy.
