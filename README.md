# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: January 18, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how PromoApp ("we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, and shares your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services (the "App").

## 1. Information We Collect

### Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Information**: Your name, email address, phone number, and password when you create an account.
- **Profile Information**: Your profile picture, preferences, and other details you add to your user profile.
- **Booking Information**: Details about products you book through the App, including store name, price, and date.
- **Communication Information**: Messages you send to stores or to our customer support.

### Information Collected Automatically
- **Location Data**: We collect your precise or approximate location information with your permission to show you nearby malls and stores.
- **Device Information**: Information about the device you use to access the App, including device model, operating system, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with our App, such as the stores you view and the features you use.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- Process your bookings and redemptions.
- Show you relevant malls, stores, and promotions near your location.
- Communicate with you about your account, bookings, and promotional offers.
- Facilitate communication between you and store owners.
- Detect, investigate, and prevent fraudulent transactions and other illegal activities.

## 3. Sharing of Information
We may share your information with:
- **Store Owners**: When you book a product, we share your name and booking details with the respective store owner to facilitate the transaction.
- **Service Providers**: Third-party vendors who perform services on our behalf, such as cloud hosting, analytics, and customer support.
- **Legal Requirements**: If required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

## 4. Your Choices and Rights

### Location Information
You can choose whether or not to allow the App to collect and use real-time information about your device's location through the device's privacy settings. If you disable location services, some features of the App (like finding nearby stores) may not function properly.

### Account Information
You may update or correct your account information at any time by logging into your account settings in the App.

### Data Deletion
You can request the deletion of your account and personal data by contacting us through the support channel provided in the App.

## 5. Security
We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, and destruction.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
